About the studio

Bespoke programmes,
not a class timetable.

Fred's Gym is located in Hampstead, North West London, just a stone's throw from Hampstead underground station. Every client is treated as an individual who needs a programme built around their own goals — not a generic plan.

That means dedicated one-to-one sessions with a choice of uniquely skilled personal trainers, each bringing their own specialism into the room: from sports-injury rehabilitation and osteopathy to nutrition, pilates and strength & conditioning.

Why one-to-one

Consistency beats intensity.

"You do not need to be perfect but you do need to be consistent" — it's a line one of the trainers uses often, and it sums up how sessions here are built: sustainable programming around your own movement, injuries and goals, reviewed and adjusted as you go rather than run off a fixed class plan.
