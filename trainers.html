Every trainer at Fred's runs their own one-to-one programme rather than a shared class plan. Between them they cover sports-injury rehabilitation, osteopathy, nutrition, pilates, strength & conditioning, kettlebell work, pre/post-natal training and more — full qualifications are listed on every profile below.
